CONDITIONS FOR CANCELLATION

Cancellations on order placed are allowed only before order is shipped from Royal furniture warehouse, we will accept the cancellation with deduction of Payment Gateway processing and service fee of 2% on the paid value for the transaction. For more details please contact our customer care to assist with the same.

No cancellations are allowed once the order is shipped from Royal Furniture warehouse. In case of any exception, at the sole discretion of Royal Furniture, two-way shipping charges will be borne by the customer. For more details please contact our customer care to assist with the same.

In case of cancellations, no refund will be made for Part Payment orders that contain products from all categories, the advance amount will be considered as cancellation charges. For more details please contact our customer care to assist with the same.

Please notify us of receipt of a Damaged/ Defective product maximum within 24 hours of delivery with a proof i.e. with images or videos. For more details please contact our customer care to assist with the same.

Please do not accept any products with damaged/ tampered packaging (excludes normal wear & tear and markings on the outer surface of the package, acceptable in the transit process).

Products/ Items should be UNUSED.

Products should be returned in their original packaging along with the original price tags, labels, and invoices.

It is advised that the return packets should be strongly and adequately packaged so that there is no damage to goods in transit.

Any products damaged post delivery to the customer, shall not be accepted for return.

CONDITIONS FOR RETURN:

Items can only be returned in case, It has been delivered in damaged condition or there is some issue with the finishing of the product.

*Please note: We do not allow returns on the products sold in sale or discount. Only regular price items will be eligible for returns until and unless they're unused and have not been removed from its original packaging.

Royal Furniture has all rights to reject returns for any order.

Returns are only accepted for the following reasons:

  • Damaged Products
  • Manufacturing defects
  • Incomplete Products
  • Incorrect Product

    • PROCESS FOR INITIATING RETURN:

    If you're a guest user or a registered user, then please contact us to initiate your return request.

    Royal Furniture will pick up the product that you wish to return from your doorstep; However, locations where we do not have reverse pickup facility, We may request you to self ship the product to us or dispose/destroy them.

    Please note: Customized items cannot be returned.

    CONDITIONS FOR REFUNDS:

    Refunds will be processed within 7 business days* of receipt of the product by Royal Furniure, provided goods are received in acceptable condition, as dispatched from our warehouse. Refund method will be based on the mode of order payment, as follows-

    Orders paid for through credit/ debit card will be refunded back to the credit/ debit card Orders paid for through net banking accounts will be credited back to the bank account Orders paid for through Royal Furniture Gift Certificates/ Vouchers will be refunded in form of a fresh Gift Certificate/ Voucher of the same value Orders paid for through other modes of payment will be refunded either through an account payee cheque in the customer's name (as given in "billing name" at time of purchase), or an online transfer via NEFT to customer's bank account

    If you have made any partial payment/token amount for your order to be shipped on Cash On Delivery and in any case, it was undelivered or you refused the delivery of your order than your token amount will be freezed and cannot be refunded to you until next order. **Please note: you will be able to use that amount in your next order and your next order will be accepted only if you make full advance payment for your order amount and token amount will be adjusted accordingly. For more information, Please contact our support team at Info@Royalfurniutre.com

    * Please follow up with your respective bank/ card issuer post completion of refunds process by Royal Furniture, to check on reflection of funds in your account. In case of cheque refunds, additional time may be required to ship the cheque to the customer.

    If your return request is accepted, Return will be processed within 48 hours. Depending on the mode of payment you used, Banks and Payment Gateways, Usually takes 7-10 working days to credit the amount to your account.

    In case, You have used your wallet credits to make purchase, Than your used wallet credits will be reimbursed to your wallet.

    Certain instances in which returns cannot be accepted

    The product is damaged due to misuse The product being incidentally damaged due to non-reporting of an instance of the previous malfunctioning Any sort of damage or defect which is not warranted by the manufacturer Any product which is not returned with all the original packaging and all the accessories inclusive of box & packaging from manufacturer's side, if any, and each and every item originally included with the product(s) delivered.
